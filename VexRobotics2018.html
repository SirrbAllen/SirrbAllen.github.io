Vex Robotics 2017 - 18

Project Details

The Vex Robotics Competition is a worldwide, yearly challenge that tasks teams to build and program a robot to complete tasks. The 2017-18 game consists of cones that must be stacked on 'mobile goals' which must then be moved into goal zones.

This was the first time I took part in this competition and me and my team was sucessful in qualifying for the national championships!

Our Robot

The robot this year was designed to pickup and stack cones. The idea is to take the cones and place them on top of mobile goals. Then, using a mechanism similar to a forklift truck, it lifts these mobile goals and places them in scoring zones.

Awards

During the competitions we went to we managed to win multiple awards:

  • Tournament Champion - Won for beating all of the opposition at a regional competition

  • Think Award - Won for excellent programming skills
