Vex Robotics 2018 - 19

Project Details

The Vex Robotics Competition is a worldwide, yearly challenge that tasks teams to build and program a robot to complete tasks. The 2018-19 game consists of multiple game components and complex tasks that required careful planning and lots of thought.

After huge amounts of work this year we successfully managed to qualify for the world championships by winning the national competition!

Our Robot

My teams robot was designed specifically around firing balls at the flags. We decided this would be the best idea due to the points that we would be able to score. We ended up creating a flywheel that allowed us to reliably fire the balls quickly and efficiently. We also managed to implement a “cap flipping” arm that allowed us to turn over the plastic disks onto our team colour.

Awards

During the competitions we went to we managed to win multiple awards:

  • Tournament Champion - Won for beating all of the opposition at a regional competition
  • Division Champion - Won for beating all of the other teams in our division at the nationals.
  • Think Award - Won for excellent programming skills.
